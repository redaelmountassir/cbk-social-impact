Covid-19

and Racial and Ethnic Disparities

The racial and ethnic minority groups are disproportionately being affected by the Covid-19 pandemic. This is not unique to the recent pandemic as social determinants of health have been evident throughout history for a long time. Discrimination, lack of access of healthcare, occupations on the frontlines, and educational, income, and wealth gaps together can put these people at a significantly higher risk of being hospitalized or dying.
